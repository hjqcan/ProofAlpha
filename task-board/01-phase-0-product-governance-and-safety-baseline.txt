Phase 0: Product Governance and Safety Baseline
===============================================

Status
------
[DONE] Accepted by baseline product docs, task-board source-of-truth files, and
`docs/product/autotrade-phase-0-quality-gate.md`.


Intent
------
Freeze the product direction and safety baseline before expanding the control
room. This phase turns the current rough product shape into a governed build
plan with measurable gates.


Core Boundary
-------------
This phase does not add trading behavior. It defines product guardrails,
acceptance evidence, and repository-wide safety contracts that later phases must
honor.


Accepted Scope
--------------
- current product inventory
- explicit Paper, Live, and command-mode policy
- product safety invariants
- test and evidence baseline
- roadmap source-of-truth links


Out of Scope
------------
- new strategies
- UI redesign
- real-money execution testing
- credential management changes beyond policy and tests


Acceptance Criteria
-------------------
1. Current backend, API, CLI, and Web App surfaces are inventoried.
2. Product safety invariants are documented and testable.
3. Existing build and test commands are captured as the minimum quality gate.
4. Phase task board becomes the source of truth for product hardening order.
5. No implementation phase can claim completion without evidence paths.


Tasks
-----
[DONE] P0-T001 Inventory current product surfaces and risks.
[DONE] P0-T002 Freeze safety policy contracts.
[DONE] P0-T003 Establish build, test, and acceptance evidence baseline.
[DONE] P0-T004 Publish roadmap source of truth and update rules.
