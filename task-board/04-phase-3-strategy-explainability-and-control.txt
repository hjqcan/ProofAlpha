Phase 3: Strategy Explainability and Control
============================================

Status
------
[DONE]


Intent
------
Move strategies from black-box background workers to inspectable, controllable
product units. Operators must see what each strategy is trying to do, why it is
blocked, and why it made or rejected a decision.


Core Boundary
-------------
Explainability must be grounded in real strategy state and decision records. Do
not invent UI-only explanations that cannot be audited from persisted data.


Accepted Scope
--------------
- strategy detail contract
- last decision reason chain
- blocked reason model
- parameter version, diff, and rollback workflow
- strategy control UI polish


Out of Scope
------------
- new alpha strategies
- ML forecasting
- direct UI order placement
- undocumented parameter mutation


Acceptance Criteria
-------------------
1. Every strategy detail shows lifecycle state, desired state, health, recent
   error, recent decision, blocked reason, and key parameters.
2. Decision records link market data, signal, risk verdict, order intent, and
   final action where available.
3. Parameter changes are versioned, audited, validated, and reversible.
4. Run/Pause/Stop controls explain why they are disabled.
5. Strategy regression tests cover state transitions and command audit.
