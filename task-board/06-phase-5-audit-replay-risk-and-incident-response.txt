Phase 5: Audit Replay, Risk, and Incident Response
==================================================

Status
------
[DONE]


Intent
------
Give operators a reliable way to answer what happened, why it happened, and
what the system did in response. Risk events and audit replay become first-class
product workflows.


Core Boundary
-------------
Incident response must not hide, rewrite, or delete audit evidence. Replay is a
read-only reconstruction path unless a separate audited operator command is
issued.


Accepted Scope
--------------
- audit timeline contract
- risk event drill-down
- unhedged exposure timeline
- incident response actions and runbook
- replay export


Out of Scope
------------
- automated legal or tax reporting
- mutable audit history
- forced Live liquidation without existing risk policy
- replacing database backups


Acceptance Criteria
-------------------
1. A user can replay a market, strategy, order, or risk event from persisted
   evidence.
2. Risk events show trigger, metrics, limits, action, and affected orders.
3. Unhedged exposure records are visible with duration, market, strategy, and
   mitigation result.
4. Incident response actions are explicit, audited, and reversible where
   reversal is safe.
5. Exported replay packages are sufficient for offline review.
