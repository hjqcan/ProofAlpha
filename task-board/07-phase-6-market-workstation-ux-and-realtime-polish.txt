Phase 6: Market Workstation UX and Realtime Polish
==================================================

Status
------
[DONE]


Intent
------
Polish the Web App into a focused trading workstation: clear market discovery,
fresh order-book state, readable copy, responsive layouts, and trustworthy
visual states.


Core Boundary
-------------
This phase improves operator usability without adding unsupported manual trading
or bypassing strategy/risk controls.


Accepted Scope
--------------
- market discovery ranking and filters
- order-book freshness and stale-data states
- detail and activity layout polish
- i18n copy and encoding repair
- visual regression smoke


Out of Scope
------------
- marketing landing page
- decorative dashboard-only charts
- direct unmanaged order ticket
- hosted analytics


Acceptance Criteria
-------------------
1. zh-CN and en-US copy render correctly with no mojibake.
2. Market cards expose why a market is interesting or unsuitable.
3. Order book freshness is visible and stale data cannot be mistaken for live
   data.
4. Layouts work on desktop and mobile without text overlap.
5. Browser smoke captures screenshots for key views.


Tasks
-----
[DONE] P6-T001 Improve market discovery ranking and unsuitable reasons.
[DONE] P6-T002 Add order-book freshness and realtime states.
[DONE] P6-T003 Polish trade workspace layout and activity surfaces.
[DONE] P6-T004 Fix i18n copy, encoding, and operator wording.
[DONE] P6-T005 Add browser visual regression smoke.
