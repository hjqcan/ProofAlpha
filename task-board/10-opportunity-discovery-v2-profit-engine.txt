OpportunityDiscovery v2: Profit-Driven Autonomous Opportunity Engine
====================================================================

Purpose
-------
Upgrade OpportunityDiscovery from an LLM research draft workflow into a
calibrated, replayable, risk-gated opportunity engine that can eventually publish
very small Live allocations after deterministic validation.

This board does not promise guaranteed profit. It defines the engineering work
needed to measure whether an opportunity class has positive expected value after
fees, slippage, liquidity limits, calibration error, source conflict, and risk
constraints.


Non-Negotiable Rules
--------------------
1. Market history comes first. No opportunity may be backtested from in-memory
   order books alone.
2. Point-in-time evidence is mandatory. Backtest, shadow, paper, and Live gates
   must only consume information known at the evaluated timestamp.
3. LLMs may draft hypotheses and summarize evidence. LLMs may not decide fair
   probability, sizing, publication, or execution.
4. Strategy hot paths may not call LLMs, source APIs, or the internet.
5. Live publishing must pass backtest, paper, execution-quality, risk, and
   compliance gates.
6. Default Live allocation is micro-sized and cannot auto-scale.
7. Every promotion, suspension, and Live allocation must be explainable from
   persisted evidence, score, gate, and allocation records.
8. No API key, private key, paid source credential, or account secret may be
   committed.
