Intent
------
Freeze the business loop before implementation. The submission must read as a
paid agent trading gateway, not as a generic Arc integration.


Core Product Claim
------------------
ProofAlpha uses Arc to prove strategy signals and outcomes, then lets users pay
USDC for access to the agent's arbitrage opportunities or auto-trading
permissions.


Accepted Scope
--------------
- Final product one-liner.
- Subscription tiers.
- Entitlement rules.
- Demo flow.
- Explicit definition of what "strategy is useful" means.
- Explicit definition of what is testnet, simulated, or real.


Out of Scope
------------
- Guaranteed profitability.
- Real-money Live trading in demo.
- Production billing.
- Mainnet deployment.
- Replacing existing Polymarket execution.

[TODO] AH-P0-T001 Write the final one-liner and pitch paragraph.

Required one-liner:

`ProofAlpha proves alpha on Arc, sells access with USDC, and executes on
Polymarket through a risk-gated agent.`

Pitch paragraph must include:

- AI agent discovers opportunities
- Arc proves publication/outcome/reputation
- USDC subscription unlocks access
- Polymarket remains execution venue
- builder attribution makes the agent a monetizable entry point

[TODO] AH-P0-T002 Define subscription tiers.

Minimum tiers:

- `SignalViewer`
  - can view published opportunities after delay or immediately
  - no auto-trading permission
- `AutoTrader`
  - can request strategy start/copy-trade automation
  - still blocked by Paper/Live/risk/compliance gates
- `Operator`
  - can publish signals and settle revenue in demo
  - local/admin only for hackathon

Each tier needs:

- strategy ids covered
- duration
- USDC amount
- allowed API/CLI/WebApp actions
- disabled reason when missing

[TODO] AH-P0-T003 Define the strategy utility proof standard.

A strategy is "useful" in demo only if it has:

- pre-execution signal hash
- strategy/evidence/reasoning hash
- risk envelope
- Paper execution or replay outcome
- post-execution outcome record
- performance ledger entry

The proof must not imply future profit.

[TODO] AH-P0-T004 Freeze the demo flow.

Demo happy path:

1. Display unsubscribed wallet blocked from full signal details.
2. Subscribe with USDC on Arc or a labeled testnet USDC fixture.
3. Backend reads entitlement.
4. User unlocks an arbitrage opportunity.
5. Publish signal proof to Arc.
6. Execute Paper order with Polymarket builder metadata evidence.
7. Record performance outcome on Arc.
8. Record revenue settlement/split event.
9. Show agent reputation and subscriber access in WebApp.
