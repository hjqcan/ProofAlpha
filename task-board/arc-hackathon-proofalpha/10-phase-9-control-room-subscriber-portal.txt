Intent
------
Make the paid-agent loop visible in the WebApp: subscribe, unlock, inspect
proof, request automation, and review performance.


Accepted Scope
--------------
- Arc status panel.
- Wallet/subscriber input or wallet connect fixture.
- Subscription status display.
- Gated opportunity detail.
- Signal proof detail.
- Performance/reputation panel.
- Revenue/settlement panel.
- Screenshots for demo.


Out of Scope
------------
- Production auth.
- Custodial wallet.
- Complex portfolio UI.
