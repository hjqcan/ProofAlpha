Phase 9: Control Room Subscriber Portal
=======================================

Status
------
[DONE]


Intent
------
Make the paid-agent loop visible in the WebApp: subscribe, unlock, inspect
proof, request automation, and review performance.


Accepted Scope
--------------
- Arc status panel.
- Wallet/subscriber input or wallet connect fixture.
- Subscription status display.
- Gated opportunity detail.
- Signal proof detail.
- Performance/reputation panel.
- Revenue/settlement panel.
- Screenshots for demo.


Out of Scope
------------
- Production auth.
- Custodial wallet.
- Complex portfolio UI.

Completion Notes
----------------
- Subscriber identity stores a wallet fixture locally, supports plan selection,
  and refreshes backend entitlement state via `GET /api/arc/access/{wallet}/{strategy}`.
- Opportunity and signal detail panes render API-returned 403
  `ArcAccessDecision` bodies for blocked wallets and only show reasoning,
  execution policy, hashes, evidence, and transaction links after API unlock.
- Paper auto-trading uses
  `POST /api/control-room/strategies/{strategyId}/arc-paper-autotrade`; Live is
  explicitly disabled in the subscriber portal.
- Performance panel loads the selected signal outcome via
  `/api/arc/performance/signals/{signalId}/outcome` and shows the copyable
  outcome transaction hash.
- Settlement panel is honestly labeled `testnet` or `simulated`; Phase 10 owns
  durable revenue settlement backend records and split distribution.
