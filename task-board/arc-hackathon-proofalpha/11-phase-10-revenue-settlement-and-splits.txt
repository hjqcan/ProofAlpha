Phase 10: Revenue Settlement and Splits

Status

Intent
------
Show how the agent becomes a revenue-generating market entry point: subscription
fees, builder-attributed flow, and strategy/provider splits are recorded on Arc.


Accepted Scope
--------------
- Revenue settlement model.
- Event-only RevenueSettlement contract integration.
- Local revenue journal.
- Demo settlement command.
- WebApp/API read model.


Out of Scope
------------
- Production accounting.
- Tax reporting.
- Mainnet distribution.
- Actual revenue distribution unless implemented and tested.


Revenue Sources
---------------
- `SubscriptionFee`
- `BuilderAttributedFlow`
- `StrategyMarketplaceFee`
- `ManualDemoSettlement`

Recipients:

- agent owner
- strategy author
- platform
- referrer, optional
- treasury, optional
