=======================================================

Status

[TODO]


Goal
----
Make opportunity quality deterministic, auditable, and calibratable.


Scoring Formula
---------------
`netEdge = fairProbability - executableEntryPrice - feeEstimate - slippageBuffer`


Feature Areas
-------------
- evidence confidence
- official confirmation strength
- model calibration bucket
- liquidity capacity
- spread and market impact
- time half-life
- source conflict
- resolution risk
- execution freshness
- market closure and settlement ambiguity


Opportunity Types
-----------------
- information asymmetry
- order book microstructure
- cross-market or same-event consistency
- delayed repricing
- near-resolution
- liquidity mismatch


Acceptance
----------
- No positive `netEdge`, no promotion.
- No executable capacity, no promotion.
- LLM fair probability is a feature input, not final authority.
- Score breakdown is persisted and explainable.
- Calibration can compare scored probability against market-implied baseline.
