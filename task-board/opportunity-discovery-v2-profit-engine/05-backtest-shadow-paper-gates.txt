ODV2-T005: Backtest, Shadow, Paper, and Promotion Gates
=======================================================

Status
------
[DONE]


Goal
----
Create the closed validation loop before any automatic Live publication.


Gates
-----
- Backtest gate
- Shadow gate
- Paper gate
- Execution-quality gate
- Risk gate
- Compliance gate


Default Thresholds
------------------
- At least 100 evaluable samples in the same opportunity bucket, or 14 days of
  shadow/paper observation.
- Net paper PnL greater than 0.
- Brier/calibration better than market-implied baseline.
- Adverse slippage consumes no more than 25% of predicted edge.
- No critical risk event.
- No source drift or model calibration drift beyond configured threshold.


Backtest Rules
--------------
- No future evidence.
- No future price data.
- Fill model must align with historical order book depth when depth exists.
- Same replay seed and same inputs must produce same outputs.


Acceptance
----------
- A failing gate prevents `LiveEligible`.
- Gate result includes evidence ids, metrics, thresholds, and explanation.
- Paper report, slippage, PnL, risk, and live arming infrastructure are reused
  instead of duplicated.
