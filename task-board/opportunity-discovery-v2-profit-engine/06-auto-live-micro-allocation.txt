ODV2-T006: Auto Live Micro-Allocation and Kill Criteria
=======================================================

Status
------
[TODO]


Goal
----
Allow validated opportunities to publish tiny Live allocations automatically
while keeping all existing arming, risk, and compliance controls authoritative.


Default Limits
--------------
- Single order: 5 USDC.
- Single opportunity: 10 USDC.
- Single cycle: 20 USDC.
- Global active Live exposure: 100 USDC.
- Active Live opportunities: 3.


Rules
-----
- Defaults must be conservative and explicit.
- Increasing limits requires explicit config and compliance guard support.
- OpportunityDiscovery creates allocation records; Strategy and Trading execute.
- Kill criteria suspend opportunities and cancel open orders.


Kill Criteria
-------------
- realized edge below threshold
- adverse slippage above threshold
- fill rate below threshold
- drawdown above threshold
- source drift
- model calibration drift
- stale order book
- risk or compliance event


Acceptance
----------
- Auto Live cannot run unless live arming, compliance, account sync, and risk
  gates all pass.
- Suspension writes audit trail and blocks future feed publication.
- Unfilled open orders are canceled on suspension.
