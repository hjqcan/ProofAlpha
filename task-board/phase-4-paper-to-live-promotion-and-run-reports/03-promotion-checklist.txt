Phase 4.3: Promotion Checklist
==============================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Turn Paper run evidence into an explicit pass/fail checklist for Live
consideration.

Acceptance
----------
- Checklist evaluates run duration, decision coverage, stale data, risk events,
  unhedged exposure, order errors, reconciliation health, and PnL attribution.
- Failed criteria include specific reasons and affected evidence IDs.
- Passing checklist does not arm Live by itself.
- Checklist can be rendered by CLI, API, and UI.
