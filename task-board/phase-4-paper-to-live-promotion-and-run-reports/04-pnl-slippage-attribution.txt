Phase 4.4: PnL, Slippage, and Exposure Attribution
==================================================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Explain where Paper run outcomes came from.

Acceptance
----------
- Report separates realized PnL, unrealized PnL, fees if available, mark
  source, slippage estimate, latency, and stale-data contribution.
- Strategy and market attribution add up to the run totals or explain why they
  cannot.
- Unhedged exposure is measured by duration, notional, and mitigation outcome.
- Attribution calculations are covered by tests.
