Phase 5.1: Audit Timeline Contract
==================================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Create a unified timeline for decisions, orders, trades, risk events, command
events, readiness changes, and reconciliation.

Acceptance
----------
- Timeline can filter by market, strategy, order, run session, risk event, and
  time range.
- Each item includes timestamp, type, actor/source, correlation ID, and detail
  reference.
- Timeline ordering is deterministic.
- Tests cover empty, partial, and dense timelines.
