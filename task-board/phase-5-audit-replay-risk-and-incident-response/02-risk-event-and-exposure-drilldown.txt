Phase 5.2: Risk Event and Exposure Drill-Down
=============================================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Make risk triggers and unhedged exposure understandable from the control room.

Acceptance
----------
- Risk event detail shows limit, current value, threshold, state, trigger
  reason, selected action, and affected orders.
- Exposure detail shows market, outcome, strategy, notional, duration, hedge
  state, and mitigation result.
- Kill-switch activation links to triggering event when applicable.
- UI and exports use the same source data.
