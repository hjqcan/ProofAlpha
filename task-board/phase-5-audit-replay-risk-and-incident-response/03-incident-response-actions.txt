Phase 5.3: Incident Response Actions
====================================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Define and implement safe operator actions for incidents.

Acceptance
----------
- Actions include hard stop, reset, pause strategy, stop strategy, cancel open
  orders where supported, and export incident package.
- Each action has confirmation, disabled reason, audit record, and result.
- Unsafe or unsupported actions remain blocked with explanation.
- Incident runbook maps symptoms to actions without promising outcomes.
