Phase 5.4: Replay Export
========================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Package enough evidence to review an incident or trading decision offline.

Acceptance
----------
- Export includes timeline, decisions, orders, order events, trades, positions,
  risk events, strategy config version, and relevant readiness state.
- Export redacts secrets and account-sensitive material beyond what is needed
  for review.
- Export can be generated by CLI and API.
- Export schema is documented and tested.
