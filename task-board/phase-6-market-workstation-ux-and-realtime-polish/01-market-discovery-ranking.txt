Phase 6.1: Market Discovery Ranking
===================================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Help operators understand why markets are ranked, interesting, or unsuitable.

Acceptance
----------
- Market response includes or derives reason fields for ranking and exclusion.
- Filters cover category, status, liquidity, volume, expiry, accepting orders,
  and signal score.
- UI shows unsuitable reasons without hiding the market state.
- Ranking behavior is deterministic and tested.
