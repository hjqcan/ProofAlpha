Phase 6.2: Order-Book Freshness and Realtime States
===================================================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Prevent operators from mistaking stale market data for live actionable state.

Acceptance
----------
- Order-book data includes last updated time, source, and freshness status.
- UI displays fresh, delayed, stale, empty, and error states distinctly.
- Strategy and risk views surface stale-data blockers where relevant.
- Tests cover freshness thresholds and stale-state rendering.
