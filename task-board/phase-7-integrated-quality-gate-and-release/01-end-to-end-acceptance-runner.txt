Phase 7.1: End-to-End Acceptance Runner
=======================================

Status
------
[DONE]

Goal
----
Automate the acceptance checklist enough to produce repeatable local evidence.

Acceptance
----------
- Runner executes required build, test, config validate, readiness, status, and
  export commands.
- Runner separates required gates from optional Postgres smoke tests.
- Runner writes a machine-readable report and human-readable summary.
- Failures include command, exit code, and artifact path.
